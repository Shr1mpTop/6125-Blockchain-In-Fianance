### **Stage 1: 3 Areas of Finance for Blockchain Assignment**

#### **Area 1: Trade Finance & Institutional Clearing (贸易融资与机构清算)**

**1\. Existing problems and issues (现有问题与挑战):**

* **EN:** Traditional international trade finance and cross-border clearing rely on correspondent banking networks (e.g., SWIFT) and manual documentation (like Letters of Credit). This involves multiple intermediaries, leading to slow settlement times (often T+1 to T+3), high foreign exchange spreads, trapped liquidity, and significant risks of document tampering.  
* **CN:** 传统的国际贸易融资和跨境清算依赖于代理行网络（如SWIFT）和基于纸质的手工单据（如信用证）。这涉及多个中介机构，导致结算时间缓慢（通常为 T+1 到 T+3）、高昂的外汇点差、资金流动性受限，以及极大的单据篡改风险。

**2\. How blockchain can help (区块链如何提供帮助):**

* **EN:** Blockchain provides a shared, immutable ledger that acts as a single source of truth. By using wholesale Central Bank Digital Currencies (CBDCs) or commercial bank digital money, financial institutions can achieve atomic, real-time cross-border settlement. Smart contracts can also automate conditional payments once digitized trade documents (like Bills of Lading) are verified.  
* **CN:** 区块链提供了一个共享的、不可篡改的账本，作为单一的事实来源。通过使用批发型央行数字货币（wCBDC）或商业银行数字货币，金融机构可以实现原子化的实时跨境结算。一旦数字化的贸易单据（如提单）被验证，智能合约还可以自动执行条件支付。

**3\. Real-World Case Study (真实世界案例):**

* **EN:** **Partior**. It is a blockchain-based interbank clearing and settlement network jointly established by DBS Bank, J.P. Morgan, and Temasek. It enables banks to settle cross-border transactions in different currencies in real-time using commercial bank digital money, significantly reducing settlement delays.  
* **CN:** **Partior** 。这是一个由星展银行、摩根大通和淡马锡联合成立的基于区块链的银行间清算和结算网络 。它使银行能够使用商业银行数字货币实时结算不同货币的跨境交易 ，从而大幅减少结算延迟。

#### **Area 2: Parametric Insurance (参数型保险)**

**1\. Existing problems and issues (现有问题与挑战):**

* **EN:** The traditional insurance claims process is notoriously cumbersome and opaque. In sectors like agriculture or travel, policyholders must manually submit evidence of loss, which is followed by time-consuming manual assessments by adjusters. This results in delayed payouts, high administrative costs for the insurer, and frequent disputes over payout amounts.  
* **CN:** 传统的保险理赔流程极其繁琐且不透明。在农业或旅游等领域，投保人必须手动提交损失证据，随后由理赔员进行耗时的人工评估。这导致赔付延迟、保险公司的管理成本高昂，以及频繁的理赔金额纠纷。

**2\. How blockchain can help (区块链如何提供帮助):**

* **EN:** Insurance policies can be coded as smart contracts linked to trusted off-chain data sources (Oracles). For instance, if an oracle feeds data showing a flight is delayed by \>3 hours, or a region's rainfall drops below a threshold, the smart contract executes automatically. This provides near-instant payouts directly to the user's wallet without human intervention.  
* **CN:** 保险单可以被编写为链接到可信链下数据源（预言机）的智能合约 。例如，如果预言机输入的数据显示航班延误超过3小时，或者某地区的降雨量低于阈值，智能合约将自动执行。这可以直接向用户的钱包提供近乎实时的赔付，无需人工干预。

**3\. Real-World Case Study (真实世界案例):**

* **EN:** **Etherisc**. It is a decentralized insurance protocol that has successfully deployed parametric crop insurance in Africa. Using Chainlink oracles to fetch localized weather data, the smart contracts automatically trigger payouts to farmers' mobile wallets during severe droughts, bypassing traditional claims adjusters entirely.  
* **CN:** **Etherisc**。这是一个去中心化保险协议，已在非洲成功部署了参数型农作物保险。它使用 Chainlink 预言机获取当地天气数据，在严重干旱期间，智能合约会自动触发向农民移动钱包的赔付，完全绕过了传统的理赔员。

#### **Area 3: Asset Management & Alternative Investments (资产管理与另类投资) \- *\[Target Topic\]***

**1\. Existing problems and issues (现有问题与挑战):**

* **EN:** Premium alternative assets (such as private equity, commercial real estate, or high-yield fixed income) have extremely high capital barriers to entry, making them inaccessible to retail investors. Additionally, these markets lack secondary liquidity; ownership transfers are slow, legally complex, and require expensive intermediaries.  
* **CN:** 优质的另类资产（如私募股权、商业房地产或高收益固定收益产品）具有极高的资金准入门槛，使得散户投资者无法触及。此外，这些市场缺乏二级流动性；所有权转移缓慢、法律程序复杂，且需要昂贵的中介机构。

**2\. How blockchain can help (区块链如何提供帮助):**

* **EN:** Through Real-World Asset (RWA) tokenization, the digital ownership of high-value assets can be fractionalized, democratizing access for smaller investors. The use of decentralized networks and Automated Market Makers (AMMs) facilitates 24/7 global secondary market trading, unlocking deep liquidity. Smart contracts can also streamline asset servicing, such as automated coupon and redemption payments.  
* **CN:** 通过真实世界资产（RWA）代币化，高价值资产的数字所有权可以被“碎片化”，从而实现中小投资者的投资民主化。去中心化网络和自动做市商（AMM）的使用促进了全球全天候的二级市场交易，释放了深度流动性。智能合约还可以简化资产服务，例如自动化的息票和赎回支付 。

**3\. Real-World Case Study (真实世界案例):**

* **EN:** **Project Guardian (MAS) & Schroders**. Initiated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore , this project involves major institutions like Schroders, Citi, and Standard Chartered exploring tokenized investment vehicles. For example, Schroders and Calastone have explored tokenizing mutual funds (Variable Capital Companies \- VCC) on a public blockchain, allowing for atomic settlement and automated fund management via the Guardian Funds Framework.  
* **CN:** **Project Guardian（新加坡金管局）与施罗德集团** 。由新加坡金管局发起 ，该项目汇集了施罗德、花旗和渣打等大型机构，共同探索代币化投资工具 。例如，施罗德和Calastone探索了在公共区块链上将共同基金（可变资本公司 \- VCC）代币化，通过Guardian Funds Framework（Guardian基金框架）实现原子结算和自动化基金管理 。
