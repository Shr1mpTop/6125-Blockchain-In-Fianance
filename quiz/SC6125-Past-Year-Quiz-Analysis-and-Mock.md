# SC6125 Past-Year Quiz Analysis and High-Coverage Mock Bank

> 说明：`SC6125PastYearQuiz.pdf` 封面明确写着 previous quiz 的 format and topics are different（往年测验的格式和范围都不同）。所以本文件分成两部分：先解析去年题，再提供今年 Weeks 1-3 的高覆盖模拟题。  
> 阅读方式：所有英文题干、选项、答案模板后面都加中文翻译，考试时看英文，复习时看中文。

## 0. 今年 Quiz 策略

- 90 minutes（90 分钟）。
- 20 Multiple Choice Questions（20 道选择题），占 10%。
- 2 Essay / Short Answer Questions（2 道短答/论述题），占 30%。
- Covers Weeks 1 to 3（覆盖第 1 到第 3 讲）。

答题策略：

- MCQ 重点防 `false / not / most accurate`（错误项/不属于/最准确）。
- Essay 用 `problem -> mechanism -> benefit -> risk/trade-off`（问题 -> 机制 -> 好处 -> 风险/取舍）结构。
- 项目题记 `project name + institution + use case + asset/technology`（项目名 + 机构 + 用例 + 资产/技术）。

## 1. Past-Year Quiz 逐题解析

### Q1. Which of the following is ==false==?（以下哪项是错误的？）

Options shown（选项）：

1. ##### ==Real estate can only be tokenized via debt（房地产只能通过债务方式代币化）。==
2. Debt tokenization can give SMEs more access to funding（债务代币化可以让中小企业更容易获得融资）。
3. Investors can diversify their portfolios with fractionalized securities（投资者可以通过碎片化证券分散投资组合）。
4. DeFi protocols can make the trading of tokenized securities cheaper and more efficient（DeFi 协议可以让代币化证券交易更便宜、更高效）。

Answer shown：1。

解析：房地产不只能通过 debt（债务）代币化，也可以通过 equity-like ownership（类似股权的所有权）、fund units（基金份额）、SPV shares（特殊目的载体股份）、income rights（收益权）等结构代币化。  
今年相关性：高。RWA tokenization（真实世界资产代币化）和 fractionalized securities（碎片化证券）是 Lecture 2 核心。

### Q2. Which is the most accurate definition of Web3?（Web3 最准确的定义是什么？）

Options shown（选项）：

1. ==Empowering end users through control of data and identity（通过让终端用户控制数据和身份来赋权）。==
2. Enable sharing and linking of data across the internet（让互联网中的数据可以共享和连接）。
3. Access to information and content（获取信息和内容）。
4. Collaboration and the exchange of data and information（协作以及数据/信息交换）。

解析：Web3 强调 user empowerment（用户赋权）、control of data and identity（控制数据和身份）、blockchain-based decentralization（基于区块链的去中心化）。  
今年相关性：高。Lecture 1 明确区分 Web3 和 Web 3.0。

### Q3. Which of the following are decentralized stablecoins?（以下哪些是去中心化稳定币？）

### Q7. Which is a feature of ==CFMM== decentralized exchanges?（以下哪项是 CFMM 去中心化交易所的特征？）

Options shown（选项）：

1. There may be an accumulation of funds when the liquidity pool price does not match the market（当流动性池价格与市场价格不一致时，可能产生资金/费用积累）。
2. There is no way to profit from arbitrage between exchanges（无法通过交易所之间的套利获利）。
3. There are dependencies on external price feeds（依赖外部价格源）。
4. Liquidity providers connect to advertise prices（流动性提供者连接协议并报价）。

Answer shown：1。

解析：AMM/CFMM 通过 arbitrage（套利）让池子价格回到市场价格；LP 可赚取交易费。选项 3 更像 oracle-based derivative（依赖预言机的衍生品），选项 4 更像 reserve aggregation（储备聚合）。  
今年相关性：非常高。AMM/CFMM 是 Lecture 3 核心。

### Q11. Essay: Provide 2 examples of Bank use cases discussed in Lecture 4（论述题：提供两个银行用例）

Prompt asks for（题目要求）：

今年可套用例子：

- OCBC-LTA：conditional construction payments（有条件工程付款）；benefit = transparency and automatic disbursement（透明度与自动付款）；problem = off-chain verification（链下验证）。
- Project Guardian / Guardian Wholesale Network：tokenised capital markets products（代币化资本市场产品）；benefit = liquidity and settlement efficiency（流动性和结算效率）；problem = standards/legal interoperability（标准和法律互操作性）。

### Q12. Essay: Name three types of decentralized exchanges（论述题：说出三种去中心化交易所）

Prompt asks（题目要求）：Name three types of decentralized exchanges. For each type, explain how a pair of tokens are exchanged between two parties in point form（说出三种 DEX，并用要点说明两个参与者如何交换一对代币）。

参考答案：

1. Order book DEX（订单簿型 DEX）：
   - Makers place buy/sell orders（挂单者提交买/卖订单）。
   - Takers accept orders（吃单者接受订单）。
   - Settlement occurs through smart contract（通过智能合约结算）。

2. AMM / CFMM（自动做市商 / 常数函数做市商）：
   - Users trade against liquidity pool（用户与流动性池交易）。
   - Price is determined by invariant such as `x*y=k`（价格由 `x*y=k` 等不变量决定）。
   - Arbitrage aligns pool price with market price（套利让池子价格接近市场价）。

3. Reserve aggregation / Kyber-like model（储备聚合 / 类 Kyber 模型）：
   - Liquidity providers advertise prices（流动性提供者报价）。
   - Smart contract compares quotes（智能合约比较报价）。
   - Best offer is executed atomically（最佳报价被原子执行）。

Alternative（替代答案）：P2P / OTC protocols such as AirSwap（P2P/场外协议，如 AirSwap）。  
今年相关性：非常高。Lecture 3 核心短答。

CBDC is a direct liability of the central bank. Wholesale CBDC can improve interbank and cross-border settlement, while retail CBDC is digital cash for the public. Singapore does not see retail CBDC issuance as urgent because digital payments are already efficient, inclusion benefits are limited and foreign currency substitution risk is low. However, Project Orchid builds capabilities for future digital SGD use cases.

中文翻译：  
MAS 认为稳定币结合了法币可信度和区块链优势，但也可能带来发行人挤兑等金融稳定风险，因此需要监管。CBDC 是央行的直接负债。批发型 CBDC 可以改善银行间和跨境结算，零售型 CBDC 则是面向公众的数字现金。新加坡认为目前零售型 CBDC 不紧急，因为数字支付已很高效，金融普惠收益有限，外币替代风险较低。但 Project Orchid 正在为未来数字新元用例建设能力。

### SA3. Explain Project Dunbar, Partior and Project Nexus（解释 Project Dunbar、Partior 和 Project Nexus）

Reference answer：  
Partior is a blockchain-based interbank clearing and settlement network inspired by Project Ubin and established by DBS, J.P. Morgan and Temasek. It enables real-time cross-border settlement using commercial bank digital money or wholesale CBDCs. Project Dunbar is a BIS/MAS multi-country blueprint for international settlement using wholesale/m-CBDCs, not retail CBDCs. Project Nexus addresses retail real-time payment linkages by creating a common blueprint for connecting countries' instant payment systems into a multilateral cross-border network.

中文翻译：  
Partior 是一个基于区块链的银行间清算和结算网络，受 Project Ubin 启发，由星展银行、摩根大通和淡马锡建立。它使用商业银行数字货币或批发型 CBDC 实现实时跨境结算。Project Dunbar 是 BIS/MAS 多国合作的国际结算蓝图，使用的是批发型/多央行数字货币，不是零售型 CBDC。Project Nexus 关注零售实时支付连接，通过统一蓝图把各国即时支付系统连接成多边跨境网络。

### SA4. Describe the RWA tokenization process（描述 RWA 代币化流程）

Reference answer：  
RWA tokenization should start with legal rights, not technology. The issuer first defines the asset and rights being tokenized, then determines regulatory classification and jurisdiction. It forms a legal vehicle such as an SPV or fund, prepares disclosures, designs token and smart contract logic, onboards compliant investors and issues tokens. After issuance, it manages lifecycle events such as audit and income distribution, and finally supports secondary trading, settlement and redemption while maintaining compliance.

中文翻译：  
RWA 代币化应从法律权利开始，而不是从技术开始。发行人首先定义被代币化的资产和权利，然后确定监管分类和司法辖区。接着建立 SPV 或基金等法律载体，准备披露文件，设计代币和智能合约逻辑，完成合规投资者准入并发行代币。发行后，需要管理审计、收益分配等生命周期事件，最后在保持合规的前提下支持二级交易、结算和赎回。

### SA5. Explain a gold-backed token architecture（解释黄金支持代币架构）

Reference answer：  
A gold-backed token represents claims on vaulted physical gold. On-chain, a GoldToken contract tracks balances, enforces transfer restrictions and supports mint/burn. An issuance/redemption controller validates mint and burn requests and enforces the 1:1 backing invariant. Governance or emergency contracts can pause transfers or upgrades. Off-chain, vault systems track bar serials and weights, auditors confirm vault totals, reserve oracles attest backing, and issuer operations coordinate legal and operational steps. Blockchain improves ledger integrity and settlement but still depends on vault, oracle, audit and legal trust.

中文翻译：  
黄金支持代币代表对金库中实物黄金的权利。链上，GoldToken 合约追踪余额、执行转让限制并支持铸造/销毁。发行/赎回控制器验证铸造和销毁请求，并执行 1:1 支持不变量。治理或紧急合约可以暂停转让或升级。链下，金库系统追踪金条序列号和重量，审计员确认金库总量，储备预言机证明支持情况，发行方运营协调法律和操作步骤。区块链提升账本完整性和结算效率，但仍依赖金库、预言机、审计和法律信任。

### SA6. Compare DAI and Terra/UST stablecoin designs（比较 DAI 和 Terra/UST 稳定币设计）

Reference answer：  
DAI is a crypto-collateralized stablecoin backed by over-collateralized assets locked in smart contracts. It uses oracles, collateral ratios, liquidation and governance to maintain solvency. It is transparent and composable but capital inefficient and complex. Terra/UST was a pure algorithmic dual-token design where UST stability depended on mint/burn arbitrage with LUNA. It was capital efficient but fragile because negative demand shocks could force mass UST redemptions into newly minted LUNA, causing dilution, LUNA price collapse and a death spiral.

中文翻译：  
DAI 是加密资产抵押稳定币，由锁定在智能合约中的超额抵押资产支持。它使用预言机、抵押率、清算和治理来维持偿付能力。它透明且可组合，但资本效率低、复杂度高。Terra/UST 是纯算法双代币设计，UST 的稳定依赖与 LUNA 的铸造/销毁套利。它资本效率高，但在负面需求冲击下很脆弱，因为大量 UST 赎回会转化为新铸造的 LUNA，导致稀释、LUNA 价格崩溃和死亡螺旋。

### SA7. Explain Terra's death spiral and propose one redesign（解释 Terra 死亡螺旋并提出一个重设计）

Reference answer：  
Terra's death spiral began when many users exited UST, especially from Anchor. UST holders burned UST to mint $1 worth of LUNA. This expanded LUNA supply, pushed down LUNA price and required even more LUNA to be minted for each UST redemption. Confidence collapsed because there was no hard redemption floor. A redesign could add circuit breakers, capped LUNA issuance, dynamic redemption haircuts, time-delayed redemption queues and rule-based exogenous reserves. The trade-off is lower capital efficiency and less instant convertibility.

中文翻译：  
Terra 的死亡螺旋始于大量用户退出 UST，尤其是从 Anchor 退出。UST 持有人销毁 UST 来铸造价值 1 美元的 LUNA。这扩大了 LUNA 供应，压低 LUNA 价格，并使每赎回一个 UST 都需要铸造更多 LUNA。由于没有硬性的赎回底价，信心崩溃。重设计可以加入熔断机制、LUNA 发行上限、动态赎回折扣、延迟赎回队列和规则化外生储备。代价是资本效率下降，且即时兑换性减弱。

### SA8. Explain AMM/CFMM and slippage（解释 AMM/CFMM 和滑点）

Reference answer：  
An AMM/CFMM is a smart contract liquidity pool that enables users to trade against reserves instead of an order book. In a constant product AMM, reserves satisfy `x*y=k`. When a trader inputs one token, the pool outputs the other token while maintaining the invariant. The trade changes reserve ratios, so the execution price differs from the initial spot price. Larger trades relative to pool depth create larger non-linear price movement, known as slippage. Arbitrageurs align pool prices with market prices.

中文翻译：  
AMM/CFMM 是智能合约流动性池，让用户不通过订单簿，而是直接与储备池交易。在常数乘积 AMM 中，储备满足 `x*y=k`。当交易者输入一种代币，池子输出另一种代币，同时保持不变量。交易会改变储备比例，因此执行价格不同于初始现货价格。交易规模相对池子深度越大，非线性价格移动越大，这就是滑点。套利者会让池子价格接近市场价格。

### SA9. Name and explain three types of DEX（说出并解释三种 DEX）

Reference answer：  
First, order book DEXs match makers and takers through orders. On-chain order books store orders in smart contracts but are costly; off-chain order books use relayers but settle on-chain. Second, AMMs let users trade against liquidity pools using invariants such as `x*y=k`, with LPs earning fees. Third, reserve aggregation protocols let liquidity providers advertise prices, and a smart contract selects the best quote for atomic execution. A fourth possible type is P2P/OTC protocols such as AirSwap, where trades are negotiated between specific parties.

中文翻译：  
第一，订单簿型 DEX 通过订单匹配挂单者和吃单者。链上订单簿把订单存在智能合约中但成本高；链下订单簿使用中继者，但在链上结算。第二，AMM 让用户使用 `x*y=k` 等不变量与流动性池交易，LP 赚取费用。第三，储备聚合协议让流动性提供者报价，由智能合约选择最佳报价并原子执行。第四类可选是 P2P/OTC 协议，如 AirSwap，交易由特定参与方协商。

### SA10. Compare CDP and pooled lending（比较 CDP 和池化借贷）

Reference answer：  
A collateralized debt position allows a user to lock collateral and mint new debt tokens without needing a lender, as in MakerDAO. It creates a stable or liquid token backed by the borrower's collateral and governed by collateral ratios and liquidation rules. Pooled lending, such as Compound or Aave, aggregates lenders' assets into smart contract pools. Borrowers borrow existing assets from the pool, and interest rates vary with utilization. CDPs create new debt tokens, while pooled lending reallocates existing assets.

中文翻译：  
抵押债务头寸允许用户锁定抵押品并铸造新的债务代币，不需要直接贷款人，MakerDAO 就是例子。它创造由借款人抵押品支持的稳定或流动代币，并由抵押率和清算规则约束。池化借贷如 Compound 或 Aave，把贷款人的资产汇集到智能合约池中。借款人从池中借已有资产，利率随利用率变化。CDP 创造新的债务代币，池化借贷重新分配已有资产。

### SA11. Explain cTokens and interest accrual（解释 cToken 和利息累积）

Reference answer：  
cTokens are ERC-20 tokens minted when users supply assets to Compound. They represent a proportional claim on the underlying asset pool and the right to redeem principal plus interest. The number of cTokens held by the user generally stays constant, while the exchange rate between cToken and underlying asset increases over time. Therefore, interest is realized when cTokens are redeemed. For example, if 50,000 cUSDC initially redeem for 1,000 USDC, they may later redeem for 1,050 USDC as the exchange rate rises.

中文翻译：  
cToken 是用户向 Compound 供应资产时铸造的 ERC-20 代币。它代表对底层资产池的按比例索取权，以及赎回本金加利息的权利。用户持有的 cToken 数量通常保持不变，而 cToken 与底层资产的兑换率随时间上升。因此，利息在赎回 cToken 时实现。例如，50,000 cUSDC 最初可赎回 1,000 USDC，之后随着兑换率上升，可能赎回 1,050 USDC。

### SA12. Discuss yield farming sources and risks（讨论 yield farming 的收益来源和风险）

Reference answer：  
Yield farming earns returns through supply interest, swap fee income and participation rewards. Supply interest comes from lending markets such as Compound or Aave. Swap fee income comes from providing liquidity to AMM pools, where LPs earn trading fees. Participation rewards come from liquidity mining or governance token incentives. Risks include flash loan attacks, rug pulls, reentrancy attacks, key exploits, oracle manipulation and unsustainable token rewards. High APY should be evaluated by asking whether yield comes from real economic activity or temporary incentives.

中文翻译：  
Yield farming 的收益来自供应利息、交易费收入和参与奖励。供应利息来自 Compound 或 Aave 等借贷市场。交易费收入来自向 AMM 池提供流动性，LP 赚取交易费。参与奖励来自流动性挖矿或治理代币激励。风险包括闪电贷攻击、rug pull（卷款跑路）、重入攻击、密钥漏洞、预言机操纵和不可持续的代币奖励。看到高 APY 时，应判断收益来自真实经济活动还是临时激励。
